01Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how you use the Services, this may include:

Information you provide

  • Contact information. If you email us, book a session, or otherwise reach out, we receive the information you choose to share, such as your name and email address.
  • Preferences. Settings you choose within the Dot 1 News app, such as saved articles or display preferences, may be stored on your device.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data. Like most digital services, we may receive basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, and general usage activity, used to keep the Services working reliably.
  • Diagnostics. Crash and performance data may be collected to help us identify and fix problems.

The Dot 1 News app is a news reading experience. It does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for sensitive personal information to use it.

02How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services;
  • Deliver and improve news content and app features;
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve performance and reliability;
  • Respond to your questions, requests, and correspondence;
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Services and comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it to build advertising profiles about you.

03How we share information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers. Trusted vendors who help us operate the Services (for example, hosting and analytics providers) may process information on our behalf, under obligations to protect it.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Dot One Media, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers. If our business is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

News articles and links may direct you to third-party websites; once you leave our Services, this Privacy Policy no longer applies, and we encourage you to review the privacy policies of those sites.

The Services are intended for a general audience and are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.
