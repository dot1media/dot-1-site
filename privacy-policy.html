01Categories of personal data collected

Apple Data Safety: “Data Collected.”

A. Data you may provide voluntarily

  • Email address (if account features or contact forms are enabled)
  • Display name or username (if applicable)
  • Communications you send to us (feedback, inquiries)

Purpose: Account functionality, communication, support
Apple classification: Contact Info
Linked to user: Yes (only if an account exists)

B. Data collected automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage data (article views, session duration)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • IP address (used transiently for security and analytics)

Purpose: App functionality, analytics, security
Apple classification: Diagnostics
Linked to user: No (aggregated / anonymized)

C. Data explicitly NOT collected

Dot 1 News does not collect:

  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos, audio, or files
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Health data
  • Sensitive personal identifiers

03Legal basis for processing (GDPR — Article 6)

Dot 1 News processes personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — when you voluntarily provide information
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — app security, performance, editorial integrity
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — compliance with applicable laws

We do not process personal data for automated decision-making or profiling under Article 22.

04Use of information

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Improve stability and performance
  • Respond to user communications
  • Protect platform integrity
  • Fulfill legal and regulatory obligations

Dot 1 News does not sell or monetize personal data.

05Editorial scoring & non-profiling notice

Dot 1 News applies a proprietary Four-Lens Content Scoring Model:

  • Biblical Alignment
  • Prophetic Significance
  • Source Credibility
  • Humanities Impact

These scores:

  • Are editorial judgments, not algorithmic profiling
  • Are not personalized
  • Do not rely on user behavior or identity
  • Do not constitute automated decision-making under GDPR

06Data sharing & disclosure

We may share limited data with:

  • Infrastructure and hosting providers
  • Security and analytics vendors (privacy-compliant)

All third parties are contractually bound to:

  • Use data only as instructed
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Comply with applicable data protection laws

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

07Data retention

Personal data is retained:

  • Only as long as necessary for the stated purposes
  • Or as required by law

Users may request deletion at any time.

08User rights

GDPR (EEA / UK users)

You have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15)
  • Rectification (Art. 16)
  • Erasure (Art. 17)
  • Restriction (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20)
  • Objection (Art. 21)

CCPA / CPRA (California residents)

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is collected
  • Request deletion
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Opt out of sale or sharing (note: Dot 1 News does not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Authorized agent requests: Permitted with verification.

09Children’s privacy

Dot 1 News does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (COPPA). If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly.
